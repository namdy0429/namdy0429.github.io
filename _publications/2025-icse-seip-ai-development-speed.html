--- layout: publication year: 2025 title: "How much does AI impact development speed? An enterprise-based randomized controlled trial" authors: - Elise Paradis - Kate Grey - Quinn Madison - Daye Nam - Andrew Macvean - Vahid Meimand - Nan Zhang - Ben Ferrari-Church - Satish Chandra venue: ICSE SEIP '25 venue_tags: - ICSE type: - Peer-reviewed Conference tags: - Software Engineering - AI-Assisted Development - Productivity - Empirical Study pdf: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2410.12944 --- A randomized controlled trial with 96 full-time Google software engineers studying the impact of AI features on developer productivity. The study found that AI significantly shortened the time developers spent on complex tasks, with developers who spend more hours on code-related activities being faster when using AI assistance.
