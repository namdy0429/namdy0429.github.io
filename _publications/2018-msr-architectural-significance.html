--- layout: publication year: 2018 title: "Toward predicting architectural significance of implementation issues" authors: - Arman Shahbazian - Daye Nam - Nenad Medvidovic venue: MSR '18 venue_tags: - MSR type: - Peer-reviewed Conference tags: - Software Architecture - Mining Software Repositories - Software Engineering - Prediction pdf: https://dl.acm.org/doi/pdf/10.1145/3196398.3196440 slides: /talks/Predictar.pdf --- In a software system's development lifecycle, engineers make numerous design decisions that subsequently cause architectural change in the system. This paper investigates predicting the architectural significance of implementation issues to help identify when maintenance tasks may lead to unintentional architectural changes.
